About IRA

IRA is not just another assistant — it is a Holistic Alignment Companion. Built with empathy and intelligence, IRA is designed to walk beside you across every dimension of your life:

🧠 Mental Wellness

Understand your cognitive load, stress triggers, and thought patterns. IRA helps declutter your mind with focus sessions, mental journaling, and reflective conversations.

❤️ Emotional Awareness

Track your moods daily. IRA recognizes emotional patterns and offers support through journaling prompts, meditative nudges, or a listening presence.

💪 Physical Health

IRA helps you build and maintain healthy routines — whether it's hydration reminders, tracking sleep, or nudging you to move during long sessions.

🛐 Spiritual Growth

IRA adapts to your belief system and gently nudges you toward deeper spiritual practices — be it prayer, reflection, meditation, or scripture. It supports spiritual streaks and even welcomes your doubts.

💼 Financial & Career Progress

Track your career goals and financial well-being. IRA suggests job opportunities, skills to learn, and helps plan your financial future with intention.

💬 Intelligent Companion

The core of IRA is a smart, contextual chatbot that knows your mood, calendar, goals, and spiritual rhythm. It doesn't just respond — it reflects and plans with you.

🔔 Proactive Nudging

Based on your patterns, IRA nudges you throughout the day — not to overwhelm, but to realign. These nudges are soft, respectful, and built around consent.

🎤 Voice-Enabled

You can talk to IRA using your voice. Speak your mood, ask a question, or just reflect aloud — and IRA listens and responds like a companion would.

📅 Calendar-Aware

Link your Google Calendar and IRA adapts — avoiding busy hours, inserting reflection gaps, and planning your wellness without disrupting your schedule.

📈 Dynamic Reports

Receive personalized Morning, Midday, and Evening reports. These reflections help you stay grounded, focused, and aligned throughout the day.

IRA is your mirror, your coach, your spiritual ally — all in one place. Built for growth, grounded in compassion, and designed to help you become your truest self.
